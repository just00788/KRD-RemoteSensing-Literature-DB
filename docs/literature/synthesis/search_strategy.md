# Search strategy and limitations

Created: 2026-07-07

Scope requested: 2000-2026 Chinese + English papers related to rocky desertification / karst rocky desertification / desertification degree extraction or classification.

## Inclusion priority
1. Direct KRD / rocky desertification extraction, monitoring, classification, degree grading.
2. Bare rock fraction / exposed bedrock fraction / fBR / FBR retrieval in karst regions.
3. Multi-source remote sensing + ML/DL/feature space/LSMM/MESMA/SMA/SVM/RF/DeepLabV3+/U-Net/FCN/GEE.
4. Broader desertification classification papers only when methodologically useful.

## Databases strongly recommended for final manual completion
Because many Chinese papers and theses are behind CNKI/Wanfang/VIP and not completely indexed in public web search, final exhaustive completion should be checked with:
- CNKI advanced search: 主题=("石漠化" AND "遥感" AND ("分类" OR "提取" OR "裸岩率" OR "基岩裸露率"))
- CNKI advanced search: 主题=("石漠化" AND ("随机森林" OR "支持向量机" OR "机器学习" OR "深度学习" OR "DeepLabV3+" OR "语义分割"))
- Wanfang: 石漠化 遥感 分类 裸岩率 基岩裸露率
- Web of Science/Scopus: TS=("karst rocky desertification" OR "rocky desertification") AND TS=(remote sensing OR classification OR extraction OR mapping OR monitoring OR "bare rock fraction" OR "exposed bedrock")
- Google Scholar: exact phrase queries listed above.

## Important limitation
This package is a comprehensive, web-verified and candidate-augmented bibliography. It should not be described as mathematically exhaustive, because CNKI/Wanfang/paywalled databases may contain records not visible to public web search.
