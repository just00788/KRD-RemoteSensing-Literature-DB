# Reading map for your Remote Sensing paper

## For Introduction
Use:
- EN017 for review framing and current challenges.
- CN005 for Chinese methods review.
- EN001, EN002, EN003, EN009, EN010 to position multi-source data, fBR/FBR, ML and feature-space classification.
- EN004, EN005, CN009, CN010 for bare rock fraction/exposed bedrock physical indicators.

## For FBR / LSMM / MESMA methods
Use:
- EN002 (MESMA and fVC/fBR sensor comparison)
- EN004 (DPM/SMA/MESMA comparison at sub-pixel scales)
- EN005 (KBRI and exposed bedrock fraction)
- EN007 (spectral indices + MESMA + SVM)
- CN009 (remote sensing indicators for KRD)
- CN012/CN013 as Chinese UAV + bare-rock rate examples, after verifying details in CNKI/Wanfang.

## For ML classification baselines
Use:
- EN001 (RF/BDT/ERT multi-source comparison)
- EN003 (RF with MIC/correlation feature selection)
- CN001 (Sentinel-2 + DEM + RF feature optimization)
- CN002 (optimized SVM and feature selection)
- EN007 (SVM with MESMA/indices)

## For Deep learning / semantic segmentation
Use:
- EN008 (deep learning image segmentation for KRD severity levels)
- CN003 (improved DeepLabV3+ bare rock extraction)
- General method references: CAND010, CAND011, CAND012 for FCN/DeepLabV3+/U-Net.

## For DEM/topographic constraints
Use:
- CN001 (DEM + Sentinel-2 feature set)
- CN002 (slope and bare rock/FVC contributions)
- EN020 (spectral-texture-scattering-terrain feature set)
- EN004/EN005 for topographic/heterogeneous landscape difficulties.
- Your own openDEM + GF-7 stereo DEM experiment will be stronger than most prior papers if reported carefully.

## For results/discussion comparisons
- Compare GF/Sentinel/Landsat performance with EN001 and EN002.
- Discuss LSMM/MESMA/FBR limitations with EN002, EN004, EN005, EN007.
- Discuss spatial resolution and semantic segmentation advantages with EN001, EN008, CN003.
- Discuss temporal monitoring and generalization with EN011, EN012, EN013, EN015, EN016, EN018.
