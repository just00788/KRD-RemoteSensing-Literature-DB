# Remote Sensing Terminology

## When to use this reference

Use this reference when normalizing terms, translating Chinese KRD concepts, or checking consistency across a manuscript.

## Preferred Terms

- karst rocky desertification (KRD)
- rocky desertification
- bare-rock fraction (FBR)
- exposed bedrock fraction (EBF)
- spectral mixture analysis (SMA)
- linear spectral mixture model (LSMM)
- multiple endmember spectral mixture analysis (MESMA)
- semantic segmentation
- DEM-derived topographic factors
- cross-region transfer
- spatial block split
- class-level accuracy

## Chinese-English Mapping

- 石漠化: rocky desertification; use karst rocky desertification when carbonate karst context is explicit.
- 裸岩率: bare-rock fraction or exposed bedrock fraction.
- 基岩裸露率: exposed bedrock fraction.
- 喀斯特石漠化: karst rocky desertification.
- 地形因子: topographic factors.
- 迁移验证: transfer validation or cross-region validation.

## Metric Terms

Continuous FBR:

- coefficient of determination (R2)
- root mean square error (RMSE)
- mean absolute error (MAE)
- bias
- regression slope

Classification/segmentation:

- overall accuracy (OA)
- Kappa coefficient
- Precision
- Recall
- F1-score
- Intersection over Union (IoU)
- mean F1-score (mF1)
- mean Intersection over Union (mIoU)

## Terms to Avoid

- "rock desert class" for KRD levels.
- "classification accuracy" for a continuous FBR map.
- "GF-7 transfer DEM" unless the experiment actually supports that claim.
